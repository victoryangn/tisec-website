泰世科技有限公司（简称"泰世"）成立于深圳，是一家专注于军用防护装备和战术装备研发、生产与出口的高科技企业。

公司产品线涵盖防弹头盔、防弹衣、防弹板、防刺服、战术服装、军用帐篷、战术背包、夜视设备、防弹车辆以及高性能防护面料等多个领域。凭借卓越的产品品质和完善的售后服务，泰世的产品已远销全球50多个国家和地区，赢得了各国军警部门和安防机构的广泛认可。

公司始终坚持"品质第一、客户至上"的经营理念，严格按照国际质量管理体系运作，所有产品均通过相关国际认证。我们拥有一支经验丰富的研发团队，可根据客户需求提供定制化的防护解决方案。

{{- else -}}

TISEC TECH LIMITED ("TISEC") is a high-tech enterprise based in Shenzhen, China, specializing in the R&D, production, and export of military protective equipment and tactical gear.

Our product line covers bulletproof helmets, body armor, ballistic plates, stab-resistant vests, tactical clothing, military tents, tactical backpacks, night vision devices, armored vehicles, and high-performance protective fabrics. With superior product quality and comprehensive after-sales service, TISEC products have been exported to over 50 countries and regions worldwide, earning wide recognition from military, police, and security agencies.

Adhering to the philosophy of "Quality First, Customer Supreme," we operate under strict international quality management systems, and all products carry relevant international certifications. Our experienced R&D team provides customized protection solutions to meet specific client requirements.
