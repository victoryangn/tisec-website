## Product Overview

The HX-BV01 Advanced Bulletproof Vest is a next-generation personal protection system developed by TISEC TECH LIMITED Featuring UHMWPE soft ballistic panels combined with removable ceramic composite plates, this vest delivers exceptional ballistic protection while maintaining a lightweight, comfortable profile.

Designed and tested in strict accordance with NIJ 0101.06 and Chinese GA 141-2010 standards, the HX-BV01 provides reliable protection for military, law enforcement, and security personnel in a wide range of operational scenarios.

## Key Features

### Lightweight Design
Utilizing next-generation UHMWPE fiber technology, the HX-BV01 is approximately 30% lighter than traditional aramid fiber vests, significantly reducing fatigue during extended operations while maintaining superior ballistic performance.

### Modular Protection System
Standard-sized plate pockets accommodate interchangeable ballistic plates, allowing operators to quickly adapt protection levels (Level III to Level IV) based on mission requirements.

### Ergonomic Fit
Pattern optimized using anthropometric data for Asian body types, with adjustable side panels and shoulder straps ensuring an optimal fit and freedom of movement for operators of all sizes.

### Integrated MOLLE System
Built-in MOLLE-compatible webbing allows attachment of magazine pouches, IFAK (Individual First Aid Kit), and other tactical accessories. Internal pockets accommodate documents and communication devices.
