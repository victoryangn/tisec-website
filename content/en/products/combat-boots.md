## Product Overview

The HX-CB01 Tactical Combat Boot is a professional-grade tactical boot designed for military, law enforcement, and security personnel operating in diverse terrain and climate conditions. The upper combines premium full-grain cowhide leather with 1000D nylon for an optimal balance of protection and breathability, while the GORE-TEX lining ensures waterproof performance without sacrificing ventilation.

The natural rubber outsole with deep lug pattern provides excellent traction across all terrain types. An optional puncture-resistant midsole adds protection against sharp objects. The innovative quick-lace system with side zipper enables rapid donning and doffing — critical for emergency response scenarios.

## Key Features

### All-Terrain Performance
Deep-lug rubber outsole tested across mud, sand, gravel, and wet surfaces delivers superior grip and stability in the most challenging terrain conditions.

### Protection Meets Comfort
Reinforced toe cap protects against impact; padded collar and internal ankle support provide stability; removable Ortholite antibacterial insole keeps feet dry and comfortable during extended wear.

### Quick On/Off
Proprietary quick-lace system with side YKK zipper dual-closure design enables donning and doffing in under 5 seconds. Lace-lock mechanism prevents loosening during movement.

### Military-Grade Quality
Every pair of HX-CB01 boots undergoes rigorous tensile testing, abrasion testing, and waterproof testing. Compliant with CE EN ISO 20347 and SBP safety standards.
