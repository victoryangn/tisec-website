## About TISEC TECH

TISEC TECH LIMITED is a comprehensive enterprise specializing in the research, development, production, and sales of military protective equipment. Headquartered in Kwai Chung, NT, Hong Kong, we leverage years of industry experience and a professional technical team to provide high-quality protective products and solutions for security forces worldwide.

## Core Business Areas

Our products cover six core categories:

- **Bulletproof Equipment** — Body armor, ballistic helmets, bulletproof shields, and personal protective gear
- **Clothing & Footwear** — Military uniforms, combat boots, tactical gloves, and military apparel
- **Tents & Backpacks** — Military tents, tactical backpacks, sleeping bags, and field support equipment
- **Night Vision** — Night vision devices, thermal imaging cameras, and optical scopes
- **Military Vehicles** — Armored vehicles, patrol vehicles, and special-purpose vehicles
- **Fabrics & Textiles** — Military-grade fabrics, belts, webbing, and textile raw materials

## Global Markets

Our products are exported to the Philippines, Malaysia, Somalia, Ghana, and many other countries and regions, earning the trust and recognition of military, police, and security departments worldwide.
